Settings

This engine only affects the ✨ Tailor resume feature — job search is identical either way. Local gives keyword insertion + tips; Free AI / OpenAI / Anthropic produce precise before→after sentence rewrites of your resume. Local is the default; keys stay on your machine.
⚡ Uses a free, keyless hosted AI (Pollinations) — no signup. It's a shared public service, so it can be slower/rate-limited and your prompt (incl. resume text) is sent to it. For anything sensitive, prefer Local or your own key.
